Learn to install, wire, test and maintain solar power systems — rooftop panels, solar pumps and small solar plants. Solar energy is expanding rapidly across Maharashtra, and trained technicians are needed for installation and servicing.

This is a newer course with growing demand, especially for rooftop solar and agricultural solar pumps. Government schemes such as PM Surya Ghar and PM-KUSUM are increasing the need for skilled solar technicians across rural Maharashtra.

Welding is a practical, hands-on course with strong demand in fabrication, construction, automobile repair and manufacturing. Skilled welders are needed wherever metal is joined.

**You only need to have passed 8th standard to join this course.** If you left school early, or did not complete 10th, welding is a real path to a skilled job and a steady income. Many welders go on to run their own workshops.

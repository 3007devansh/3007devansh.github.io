Choosing the right ITI course matters — it decides the kind of work you will do
and where you can find a job. Here is a simple comparison of the four courses at
**Dekson ITI, Akola**, to help you decide.

## Electrician — for hands-on electrical work

- **Eligibility:** 10th pass · **Duration:** 2 years
- **What you learn:** wiring, motors, electrical maintenance for homes,
  factories and industry.
- **Jobs:** electrician, wireman, maintenance technician, electrical contractor,
  or your own repair business. It is also a common route into a government job
  through apprenticeship.

## COPA (Computer Operator & Programming Assistant) — for office work

- **Eligibility:** 10th pass · **Duration:** 1 year
- **What you learn:** computer operation, office software, data entry, internet,
  basics of programming.
- **Jobs:** computer operator, data entry operator, office/accounts assistant, or
  running your own computer centre / online-services shop.

## Welder — a strong choice even after 8th

- **Eligibility:** 8th pass · **Duration:** 1 year
- **What you learn:** arc, gas and MIG/TIG welding, fabrication.
- **Jobs:** welder, fabrication technician, structural steel worker, or your own
  welding workshop. **If you did not complete 10th, this is a real path to a
  skilled job.**

## Solar Technician (Electrical) — a fast-growing field

- **Eligibility:** 10th pass · **Duration:** 1 year
- **What you learn:** installing, wiring and maintaining solar panels, pumps and
  small plants.
- **Jobs:** solar installation and maintenance technician, rooftop installer, or
  your own solar servicing business. Demand is rising across rural Maharashtra
  with schemes like PM Surya Ghar and PM-KUSUM.

## How to decide

- Like working with your hands and machines? → **Electrician** or **Welder**
- Prefer office and computer work? → **COPA**
- Want a newer field with growing demand? → **Solar Technician**
- Didn't complete 10th? → **Welder** (open after 8th)

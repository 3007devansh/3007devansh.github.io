If you have just finished 10th (or 8th) and want a practical, job-oriented
course, an **Industrial Training Institute (ITI)** is one of the best paths. This
guide explains, in simple steps, how ITI admission works in Akola for the
**2026-27** session.

## Step 1 — Register on the DVET portal

Maharashtra ITI admissions are done online through the government portal
**[admission.dvet.gov.in](https://admission.dvet.gov.in)**. Create an account,
fill the admission form, and pay the small application fee. This is compulsory
for every student.

## Step 2 — Get your merit number

After the merit list is prepared, log in to see your merit numbers (State,
Category and Home District). You will also get them by SMS.

## Step 3 — Fill your option form and choose your institute

In the option form you choose which institutes and courses you want. **To choose
Dekson ITI, Akola, enter our Institute Code:**

> **Institute Code: PR27001081**

## Step 4 — Get your allotment letter

If a seat is allotted to you, you receive an SMS. Log in, download the Allotment
Letter, and print it.

## Step 5 — Report to the institute and confirm

Visit the institute with your original documents within the given dates. The
institute checks your documents, collects the fee, and confirms your admission.

## Documents you need

Every student must bring: 10th (SSC) marksheet, School Leaving Certificate
(LC/TC), Aadhaar card, Domicile/Nationality certificate, Income certificate,
bank passbook copy, and 2 passport-size photos — plus your category certificate
(caste / EWS / minority) if applicable. Keep **two photocopies** of each.

## Not sure about anything?

Admissions are open now — CAP Round 2 and Management Quota seats are available at
Dekson ITI. The easiest thing is to **call us or visit the campus**; we will look
at your marks and guide you through every step, in Marathi.

**To be filled by:** The Managing Committee / Shri Akola Gujrati Samaj
**Purpose:** To collect information about the **founding body, the Managing Committee, and decisions only the management can take.**

> **Note:** Information about the institute itself — approvals, trades, fees, campus, admissions — is collected in a **separate form (Part A — Institute Information Form)**, filled by the Principal. Please do not answer those here.

---

## Why this form matters more than you may expect

DEKSONS has **no past students, no results, and no placement record yet.** That is normal for a new institute — but it means a parent has nothing to judge us on except one thing:

> **Who is behind this college, and can they be trusted?**

The answers in this form become the most persuasive part of the website. A Samaj that has served Akola for decades and already runs other institutions is a very different proposition from a stranger opening a college. **Please answer Section 4 as fully as you can.**

---

## How to fill this form

- Answer in **English, Marathi or Gujarati** — whichever is easiest. We will translate.
- Write **"DON'T KNOW"** if unsure. Please do not guess.
- Priority: **[P0]** = urgently needed. **[P1]** = within a week. **[P2]** = later.

# Dekson ITI — Admission Marketing Campaign Plan (2026-27)

**Constraint set (from the institute):** no paid digital ads. Available assets:
**active WhatsApp/phone**, budget for **print / banners / auto-rickshaw ads**,
and **the Trust's schools & network**. Audience: 8th–10th pass students and their
parents in Akola district, mostly Marathi-speaking, on low-end phones.

---

## ⚠️ Read this first — on "student lists"

You asked whether we can get the list of students who failed 10th, or all
DVET-registered students in the district, to contact them.

**We cannot, and we should not — here is the honest position:**

- **DVET's registered-student data is confidential government data.** It is used
  for admission processing, not marketing. There is no legal way to buy or obtain
  the district roster with names/contacts for outreach, and using it would breach
  India's **DPDP Act 2023** (personal data of minors, without consent).
- **"Students who failed 10th"** is protected board/school data. Anyone selling
  such a "database" obtained it illegally; buying it exposes the institute to
  legal liability and — in a town where word travels — permanent reputational
  damage.
- **Cold-calling minors' families from a bought list is exactly the "agent"
  behaviour the website warns students against.** It would destroy the trust the
  whole site is built on.

**What the institute CAN legitimately access:**

- **Students allotted to Dekson ITI** — visible in the **institute's DVET login**
  during each CAP round (name + allotted trade). These students already chose you;
  contacting them to help complete admission is legitimate and expected.
- **Students who entered our Institute Code (PR27001081)** in their option form,
  and **Management-Quota applicants** who apply to us directly — these people give
  us their details voluntarily. This is your real first-party list.
- **Walk-ins, callers, WhatsApp enquiries, form fills** — everyone who contacts
  you. Log every one.

> **Strategy shift:** instead of chasing a list of strangers, make sure *every
> student choosing an ITI in Akola sees Dekson and reaches out first.* That is
> what the rest of this plan does — and it converts far better than cold calls.

## Channel plan (by the assets you have)

### 1. WhatsApp — your #1 free channel
- Put **9503264747** everywhere as a **click-to-chat** link:
  `https://wa.me/919503264747`. It is already the WhatsApp button on the site.
- Set a **WhatsApp Business** profile: name, address, deksons.in, hours, catalogue
  of the 4 courses, and a **greeting message** ("Namaskar! Admissions 2026-27 open.
  Which course are you interested in?").
- **Broadcast lists** (not spam): add only people who enquired or opted in
  (walk-ins, callers, students met at schools, Samaj members). Send the guide
  links (below) and admission reminders — max 1–2 a week.
- **Status updates** daily during the season: a workshop photo, "seats filling in
  Welder", a student testimonial once you have one.
- Ask the Trust's school teachers and Samaj members to **forward** the message to
  their contacts — one forward from a trusted teacher beats any ad.

### 2. The Trust's school & community network — highest conversion
- **G.S. Convent & Smt. L.D. Patel High School** and **Smt. Maherbanu College**:
  their 10th students are your most natural intake. Ask for:
  - a **notice-board poster** and a mention at the result-day gathering,
  - the principal to **link deksons.in** from the school site/Facebook (also helps
    Google — see SEO),
  - permission for a **15-minute talk** to 10th students/parents about ITI options.
- **Shri Akola Gujarati Samaj**: announce at meetings, put the link in the Samaj
  WhatsApp groups and any newsletter.

### 3. Print & outdoor (your allocated budget)
- **Pamphlet** (already printed): add a **QR code to deksons.in** on the next run,
  and hand them out at schools on/after result day, at the bus stand, and coaching
  classes. Put the **Institute Code** prominently.
- **Banners/flex** at: the campus gate, Babhulgaon road, near the bus stand, and
  outside popular schools/coaching centres (with permission).
- **Auto-rickshaw ads** — cheap and effective in Akola's semi-urban belt; wrap
  10–20 autos with the core message + phone + QR for a month around admissions.
- **Local cable / newspaper** (Lokmat, Deshonnati, Punya Nagari): a small
  classified or a short "new NCVT ITI opens in Akola" news item — the **online
  edition link is also a backlink** that helps the website rank.

### 4. Google — free, and you already have the tools
- **Google Business Profile** (under review): the moment it's live it's your
  biggest "ITI near me" win. Post weekly, add photos, gather reviews. (See
  `12-SEO-ACTION-PLAN.md`.)
- The **website + guides** now target "iti admission akola", "which iti course",
  "options after 10th" — share these guide links on WhatsApp and social; they
  answer real questions and pull students to you.

### 5. Social (free) — set up this week
- **Facebook Page + Instagram** for the institute. Post: campus/workshop photos,
  the 3 guides, admission reminders, "meet the trades" reels. Put **deksons.in** in
  the bio. Join and post (respectfully) in **Akola buy/sell/community FB & WhatsApp
  groups** and student groups.
- Send me the profile URLs → I add them to the site as `sameAs` (reinforces Google).

## Track everything in ONE sheet (free, do it)

A simple Google Sheet the office fills for **every** enquiry:
`Date · Name · Phone · Village/Taluka · Source (banner/WhatsApp/school/Google/referral) · Course interested · Status (enquired/visited/applied/admitted) · Next follow-up`

This tells you **which channel actually works**, so you spend the print budget
where it converts — and it's your legitimate, consented contact list for
follow-up.

## What NOT to do (protects the institute)

- ❌ Don't buy any "student database" / "10th fail list" / "DVET data".
- ❌ Don't cold-call numbers you didn't collect with consent.
- ❌ Don't promise "guaranteed job" or "guaranteed seat" — the site is built on
  honesty; keep every claim true.
- ❌ Don't create a second Google Business Profile — duplicates get suspended.

# DEKSONS Institute, Akola — Part A: Institute Information Form

**To be filled by:** The Principal / Head of Institute (or the officer in charge of the institute)
**Purpose:** To collect information about **the institute itself** — approvals, trades, campus, admissions and contact details — for the official website.

> **Note:** Information about the Managing Committee, Shri Akola Gujrati Samaj, and website ownership decisions is collected in a **separate form (Part B — Managing Committee Form)**. Please do not answer those here.
>
> 📎 Certificates, photographs and files to be sent are listed in two separate documents: **"Documents Checklist"** and **"Photograph Checklist"**. This form is only for questions.

---

## How to fill this form

- Answer in **English, Marathi or Gujarati** — whichever is easiest. We will translate.
- If you don't know an answer, write **"DON'T KNOW"**. Someone will find it.
- If something does not exist yet, write **"NOT YET"**. This is expected — the institute is new.
- **Please do not guess any number, date, or approval status.** A wrong fact on the website is worse than a missing one.
- Priority: **[P0]** = urgently needed, answer first. **[P1]** = needed within a week. **[P2]** = can wait.

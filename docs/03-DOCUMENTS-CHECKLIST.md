# DEKSONS Institute, Akola — Documents & Files Checklist

**Purpose:** The certificates, files and account details needed to build the website.

> 📷 **Photographs are listed separately** in the **"Photograph Checklist"** document.
> ❓ **Questions are listed separately** in **Part A (Institute Form)** and **Part B (Committee Form)**.

---

## How to send these

- **Scans or clear phone photographs** of certificates are both acceptable — as long as every word is readable.
- Send **original files**, not screenshots or WhatsApp forwards. WhatsApp badly reduces quality.
- Suggested method: a shared Google Drive folder, or a pen drive.
- Name each file clearly — e.g. `NCVT-affiliation-letter.pdf`, `logo-original.ai`.

**Priority:** **[P0]** = the website cannot launch without it. **[P1]** = needed within a week. **[P2]** = later.

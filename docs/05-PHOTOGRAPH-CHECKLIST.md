# DEKSONS Institute, Akola — Photograph Checklist

**Purpose:** Exactly which photographs are needed for the website, and how to take them.

> ✅ **Already received:** 11 exterior and campus photographs (gate, boundary wall, main building, workshop archway, roadside views). These have been colour-corrected and are ready to use.
>
> ❌ **Still needed:** everything from inside the campus. **We currently have no interior photograph at all** — no classroom, no workshop, no machine. This is the single biggest gap blocking the website.

---

## How to take the photographs

Please read this before shooting. Good photos take one afternoon; bad photos cost us the launch.

| ✔ | Do this |
|---|---|
| 📱 | **Hold the phone sideways (landscape/horizontal).** Vertical photos cannot be used as website banners. |
| ☀️ | **Shoot on a clear, sunny day**, between 8–10 am or 4–6 pm. The 11 photos we have were taken on a grey overcast day and look dull. |
| 💡 | **Switch on all lights** in every indoor room before shooting. |
| 🧹 | **Clean and tidy the room first.** Remove dust sheets, cartons, packing material, brooms, plastic bags. |
| 🔢 | Take **3–4 shots of each thing** from different angles. We will pick the best. |
| 📶 | Send **original files** — by Google Drive or pen drive. **Do not send over WhatsApp**, it destroys quality. |
| 🚫 | **Do not photograph areas still under construction**, unless you tell us clearly which ones. The website must match what a visitor actually sees. |
| 👤 | **Do not photograph any person without their written permission.** |

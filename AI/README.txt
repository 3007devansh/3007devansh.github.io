================================

WHY THIS FOLDER HAS NO AI-GENERATED PHOTOS OF THE CAMPUS
--------------------------------------------------------
You asked to AI-generate new images of the college to upload to Google Maps.
That was deliberately NOT done, for two reasons:

1. Google Business Profile policy requires photos to be AUTHENTIC and actually
   taken at the location. AI-generated or fabricated images of a real place can
   get your photos removed and your entire listing SUSPENDED. For a new institute
   building trust, that risk is not worth it.

2. Fake images mislead visitors — when the place doesn't match, you lose the
   credibility a Google presence is supposed to build.

WHAT WAS DONE INSTEAD (the legitimate, high-value version)
----------------------------------------------------------
- All 11 real photos were professionally enhanced (brighter, warmer, sharper,
  color-corrected from the flat overcast light) and saved in the /edit folder.
  GPS/EXIF data was preserved so Maps can auto-place them.
- The best photos were ranked for Google Maps (see the chat summary / below).

WHERE AI *CAN* LEGITIMATELY HELP YOUR DIGITAL FOOTPRINT
-------------------------------------------------------
Use AI for TEXT and DESIGN, not for faking building photos:
- Write the Google Business Profile description, services list, and posts.
- Draft social media captions and a simple website "About" page.
- Design a logo/banner or brochure (clearly marketing material, not a "photo").

TO GET STRONGER REAL PHOTOS (recommended shot list — shoot on a clear/sunny day,
morning 8-10am or evening 4-6pm light, avoid the grey overcast look):
- Straight-on main entrance with the DEKSONS sign, no clutter in frame
- Workshop arch, symmetrical
- Inside: classrooms, labs, workshop machinery, computer lab, library
- Students/staff in action (with consent) — these get the most engagement
- Reception / lobby
- A clean wide campus shot on a blue-sky day
- Signboard close-up

TOOLS FOR LEGITIMATE PHOTO ENHANCEMENT (if you want to do more yourself)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
- Snapseed (free, phone) — brightness, straighten, remove small distractions
- Google Photos built-in editor — quick auto-enhance
- Canva — banners, posts, brochures (design, not fake photos)
